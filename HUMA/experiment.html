Create better products & organizations for...

Users.

You devote a significant amount of time, energy, and resources to acquiring new users, but what about retaining them? You can ensure that users continue to use and love your product by making it accessible, safe, and supportive when things go wrong.

Society.

Technology has fundamentally changed human behavior in a short amount of time. Few tech companies have taken the time to discuss the potential impact their products would have on society. Now you get to be ahead of the curve.

Employees.

Great products are created by employees that feel engaged, encouraged, and included. By setting the right policies and culture, you can ensure that your organization is the kind of place people love to come to work every day.
