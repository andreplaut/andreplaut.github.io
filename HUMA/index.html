Are we doing enough to protect our users from harassment?

Where’s the line between self-expression and harassment? How do you make the call to pull down certain content, but leave other content up? These questions are challenging some of the world’s largest tech companies. You’ve worked so hard to get people to use your product, we’ll help you protect them.

Are we doing enough to make sure our product isn’t being used in illegal ways?

Russian propaganda? Money laundering? Identity theft? Malicious actors can find all sorts of ways to leverage and hijack your product. By considering these actions ahead of time, you can build in features & policies to protect yourself, your company’s mission, and your users.

Are we doing enough to make sure our employees feel safe, included, & engaged?

Look around the room at your next big meeting. Who’s sitting around the table? Who’s speaking up? Who’s getting drowned out? More diverse teams create better products, and individuals who feel safe, included, and engaged make better employees.
